EV Market Analysis Project

Introduction
The EV Market Analysis project aims to provide a detailed examination of the Electric Vehicle (EV) industry, highlighting market dynamics, growth trends, and key players. This analysis will help stakeholders understand the evolving landscape of the EV market and make informed decisions.


Objectives
1. Understand Market Growth: Analyze the factors driving the growth of the EV market.
2. Identify Key Players: Highlight the major manufacturers and their market share.
3. Assess Regional Variations: Explore how EV adoption varies across different geographic regions.
4. Evaluate Environmental Impact: Measure the environmental benefits of EVs.
5. Forecast Future Trends: Provide projections for the future of the EV market.

Data Sources
Industry reports from reputable firms.
Government publications and statistics.
Market research datasets.
Publicly available datasets.


Tools and Technologies
Power BI: For creating dashboards and interactive visualizations.
Python: For data processing and analysis.
Excel: For data organization and preliminary calculations.
SQL: for database management

Project Workflow
Data Collection: Gather data from various sources.
Data Cleaning: Process and clean the data for analysis.
Analysis: Perform data analysis using Python and Excel.
Visualization: Create visual representations of the data using Power BI.
Reporting: Compile findings into reports and presentations.

Directory Structure
data/: Contains raw and processed data files.
notebooks/: Jupyter notebooks for data analysis.
scripts/: Python scripts for data cleaning and processing.
visualizations/: Power BI dashboards and visual elements.
reports/: Final reports and presentations.
